Where do we have to put the switch? And where do you have to put the LED?
lso you have to make shure that you have enough power for the LEDs. On the box with the LEDs
you can check how much power you need for each lamp.
If you thought about this you have to prepare the materials and start s ticking the copper tape to
the template that was given.
You can tape the copper in two ways: as one continuos line or you can cut the tape to make it
look more accurate. When you decide to cut the tape you have to solder all the cutted parts, but if
you tape the copper tape as a continuos line you just have to solder the parts that are uneven. For
example when you do a rounded circuit, just like I did.
Before soldering you have to go through secruity instructions and heat your device.
